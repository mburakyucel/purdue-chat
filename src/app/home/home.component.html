About PChat
Our PChat web application seeks to be a public chat application made by and for college students. A place to have public group chats for each class, including clubs and organizations, where current and prospective students can join and interact with one another. Each group is created to facilitate easier interactions among like minded peers and be a place for discussion.
Features
  • Group chat creation and messaging
  • Direct messaging between registered members
  • Image sharing through any chat
  • Group invites and notifications
